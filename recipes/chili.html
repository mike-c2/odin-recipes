Chili

Description

This homemade chili is loaded with beef, beans, and crowd-pleasing flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 pinch chili powder
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place ground beef and onion in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir until meat is browned and onion is tender, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, stewed tomatoes with juice, and water.
  4. Season with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
